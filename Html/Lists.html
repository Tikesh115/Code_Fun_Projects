Scrambled Egg

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for delisously-rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs Butter
  • 2tbs Cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted, add eggs and cream
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
