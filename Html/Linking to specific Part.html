Film making Terms

Arch Shot
Interlude

Arch Shot

A shot in which a subject is photographed by an encircling or a moving camera.

Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to a plot, that appears in a film

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene preceding the the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue.
